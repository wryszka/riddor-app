DANGEROUS OCCURRENCE / NEAR MISS REPORT
========================================
Company: Northern Chemicals Processing Ltd
Site: Middlesbrough Plant
Report No: DO-2026-008

CLASSIFICATION: DANGEROUS OCCURRENCE

DATE AND TIME
Date: 25 March 2026
Time: 06:15 (during night shift)
Shift: Night shift (22:00 - 06:00)

LOCATION
Building: Tank Farm Area, Sector C
Specific Location: Storage Tank T-17 (Hydrochloric Acid 33%)

DESCRIPTION OF OCCURRENCE
During routine transfer of hydrochloric acid (33% concentration) from
delivery tanker to storage tank T-17, a flexible hose coupling failed
at the connection point to the fixed pipework. This resulted in an
uncontrolled release of approximately 200 litres of hydrochloric acid
onto the bunded area surrounding the tank.

The acid release created a significant vapour cloud in the immediate
area. The emergency alarm was sounded at 06:17 and the area was
evacuated. The on-site emergency response team deployed in full chemical
PPE and isolated the supply at 06:24. The spill was contained within
the bund.

PERSONS IN AREA AT TIME
- Tanker Driver: Steve Bowman (was in cab, not exposed)
- Process Operator: Lee Harrison (was at connection point, retreated immediately)
- Process Operator: Craig Foster (monitoring from control room)

INJURIES
No injuries sustained. Lee Harrison was assessed by occupational health
as a precaution - no exposure symptoms detected.

ENVIRONMENTAL IMPACT
Spill fully contained within the bund. No release to drains, watercourses
or atmosphere beyond the immediate area. Environmental officer notified.

EQUIPMENT DETAILS
Failed Component: Flexible transfer hose coupling (3" Camlock Type C)
Age of Component: 18 months (replacement schedule is 24 months)
Last Inspection: 10 January 2026 - passed visual inspection
Manufacturer: FlexiFlow Industrial Hoses

ROOT CAUSE (PRELIMINARY)
Coupling showed signs of chemical degradation to the seal. Possible
that the 24-month replacement schedule is insufficient for HCl service.

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS
1. Area evacuated and emergency response deployed
2. Supply isolated at tanker and at tank inlet valve
3. Spill contained within bund - neutralised with soda ash
4. All similar couplings on HCl lines taken out of service for inspection
5. Transfer operations suspended pending investigation
6. Environmental agency notified as precaution

Reported By: Alan Bridges (Night Shift Manager)
Countersigned: Dr. Fiona Stewart (H&S Director)
