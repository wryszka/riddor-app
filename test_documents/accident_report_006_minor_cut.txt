FIRST AID RECORD
================
Company: TechSpace Offices Ltd
Building: Innovation Hub, Floor 3
Date: 29 March 2026
Time: 11:15

INJURED PERSON
Name: Aisha Rahman
Department: Marketing
Job Title: Digital Marketing Executive

WHAT HAPPENED
Aisha was opening a delivery box containing new promotional materials
using a Stanley knife. The knife slipped and she cut the base of her
left thumb. The cut was approximately 2cm long and bled moderately.

FIRST AID GIVEN
First Aider: Pete Gardner
- Wound cleaned with saline solution
- Bleeding stopped with direct pressure
- Steri-strips applied to close the wound
- Waterproof dressing applied
- Advised to keep wound clean and dry
- Advised to see GP if signs of infection

OUTCOME
Aisha was able to return to work immediately. No hospital attendance
required. No expected absence from work.

NOTES
Reminded all staff about safe use of cutting tools. Box cutters with
retractable blades have been ordered to replace Stanley knives for
general office use.

Signed: Pete Gardner (First Aider)
Manager Informed: Yes - Sarah Knox (Marketing Manager)
