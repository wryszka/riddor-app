URGENT INCIDENT REPORT - DANGEROUS OCCURRENCE
==============================================
PRIORITY: HIGH
Company: Buildright Construction plc
Project: Riverside Apartments Phase 2
Site Address: Waterfront Drive, Manchester
Principal Contractor: Buildright Construction plc
Report No: SIR-2026-0021

INCIDENT CLASSIFICATION: DANGEROUS OCCURRENCE (SCAFFOLD COLLAPSE)

DATE AND TIME
Date: 30 March 2026
Time: 07:45 (start of shift)
Weather: Light rain, moderate wind (estimated 25-30 mph gusts)

DESCRIPTION
A section of independent scaffold on the east elevation of Block C
(levels 3-5, approximately 9-15 metres above ground) collapsed during
morning setup. The scaffold section, measuring approximately 12 metres
in length and 3 lifts in height, detached from the building and fell
outward onto the site compound area below.

The collapse occurred before operatives had accessed the scaffold for
the day's work. Two ground-level workers (see below) were in the
compound area but were approximately 20 metres from the collapse zone.

NO INJURIES were sustained.

PERSONS IN VICINITY
- Dave Hancock (Site Foreman) - at site office, 30m away
- Paul Jenkins (Labourer) - walking to stores, approximately 20m away
- Ahmed Yusuf (Labourer) - at material laydown area, approximately 25m away

SCAFFOLD DETAILS
Type: Independent scaffold (tube and fitting)
Erected by: ProScaff Solutions Ltd (subcontractor)
Date Erected: 2 weeks ago (16 March 2026)
Last Formal Inspection: 23 March 2026 by ProScaff (passed)
Scaffold Inspector: Kevin Drake (CISRS Advanced)
Ties: Reveal ties to window openings (Block C has no through-ties)

PRELIMINARY OBSERVATIONS
- Several reveal ties appear to have pulled out of the window openings
- Wind loading may have exceeded the capacity of the reveal tie arrangement
- The scaffold design drawing specified through-ties at every other lift,
  but the building structure did not permit this and reveal ties were
  substituted without formal design review
- The scaffold tag showed "SAFE TO USE" from the 23 March inspection

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS
1. Site compound area evacuated and exclusion zone established (50m)
2. All scaffold access on site suspended immediately
3. Emergency services notified (non-injury - for information)
4. HSE notified by phone: 0345 300 9923 at 08:05
5. ProScaff Solutions managing director contacted
6. All scaffold inspection records and design drawings secured
7. Photographs and drone footage captured before any recovery work
8. All other scaffolds on site to be independently inspected before use
